# MonoPod Pricing & Packaging

## Pricing Philosophy

- **Value-based pricing**: Priced based on business value delivered, not just seats
- **Transparent**: No hidden fees, clear upgrade paths
- **Flexible**: Self-service for SMBs, custom for enterprise
- **Fair**: Pay for what you use with reasonable limits

## Pricing FAQs

**Q: What happens if I exceed my API call limit?**  
A: Requests will return a 429 (Too Many Requests) error. You can purchase additional API call add-ons or upgrade your plan.

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**  
A: Yes! Upgrade anytime. Downgrade at the end of your billing period.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for annual billing?**  
A: Yes, save ~17% by paying annually.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**  
A: Yes, all paid plans have a 30-day free trial. No credit card required for Free tier.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: Credit card, PayPal, wire transfer (Enterprise only).

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Yes, full refund within 14 days if you're not satisfied.

**Q: Do you offer nonprofit discounts?**  
A: Yes, 50% off Professional and Business plans for registered nonprofits.

**Q: What's included in setup/onboarding?**  
A: Professional/Business: Self-service docs + videos. Enterprise: Dedicated onboarding with training.
